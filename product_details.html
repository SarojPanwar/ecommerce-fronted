A denim shirt is a timeless wardrobe staple known for its rugged yet stylish appeal. Crafted from durable cotton denim fabric, it typically features a button-down front, pointed collar, and chest pockets—often with button flaps. The shirt's slightly faded or stone-washed finish adds a casual, lived-in look, while the structured stitching and sturdy seams emphasize its classic workwear roots. Versatile in nature, a denim shirt can be worn on its own, layered over a t-shirt, or styled under a jacket for a more polished outfit. Its blend of comfort, durability, and effortless style makes it suitable for both casual and semi-formal settings.
